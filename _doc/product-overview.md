# Hello Orias — Product Overview

## What It Is
A minimal single-section landing page whose entire content is one clean greeting: **"Hello, Orias."** One page, one section, nothing more.

## Purpose
A personal presence / greeting page for Orias — designed to be shared as a direct, instantly memorable link. The whole point is immediate impact with zero clutter.

## Target Audience
Anyone who receives or clicks Orias's personal page link.

## Brand & Tone
- **Voice**: Direct, clean, confident
- **Aesthetic**: Airy, white-space-forward, minimal
- **Typography**: Strong display headline for the greeting, light supporting text if any

## Scope
- 1 page
- 1 section (full-screen hero / greeting)
- No navigation, no footer, no multi-section layout
- No forms, no auth, no dynamic data

## Strategic Principles
- Simplicity is the product. Every element that isn't the greeting is noise.
- Fast to load, zero distractions, immediate impression on arrival.
- Easy to update or personalize without touching layout code.
